Why Have An All-Women* Event?

Women are hugely underrepresented in tech--whether in academia, research, industry, or leadership roles. FemmeHacks strives to bring women together to create a platform for peer support, community-building, exploration, and mentorship. We want to have fun together, building products that are for us and by us. Together we are stronger than ever, and allowing for a welcoming space where exploration is encouraged will allow for huge strides in the building of a supportive community. And then when we're all the CEOs of every damn company out there, we'll laugh and reminisce at our time spent at FemmeHacks :)
